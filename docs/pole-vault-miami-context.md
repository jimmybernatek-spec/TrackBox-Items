# Pole Vault Miami Integration

TrackBox and Pole Vault Miami are closely connected.

Pole Vault Miami functions as:
- A coaching platform
- Athlete development system
- Camp and clinic structure
- Community and athlete pipeline
- Product testing environment
- Brand growth channel

TrackBox supports athletes through:
- Fuel systems
- Hydration systems
- Recovery systems
- Consumables
- Session Packs
- Meet-day preparation

## Coaching Integration
TrackBox should eventually integrate with:
- Athlete logging
- Session tracking
- Grip tracking
- Pole tracking
- Jump counts
- Meet tracking
- Recovery tracking
- Readiness systems

## Camps and Clinics
TrackBox products should integrate into:
- Camps
- Clinics
- Team training
- Meet coaching
- Pole rentals
- Recovery stations

## Athlete Development Philosophy
Athlete preparation is treated as a full system.

Focus areas:
- Recovery
- Fuel
- Hydration
- Readiness
- Consistency
- Competition preparation
- Long-term athletic development

## Long-Term Goal

- Coaching

- Products
- Recovery
- Education
- Camps
- Community
- Digital tools
