# TrackBox Brand Guide

## Brand Identity
TrackBox is a premium athlete preparation brand focused on performance, recovery, hydration, fuel, and competition readiness.

The brand should feel:
- Elite
- Athletic
- Scientific
- Modern
- Minimalist
- Dark and cinematic
- Performance-driven

## Core Messaging
Prepared Athletes Perform Differently.

Fuel. Hydrate. Recover. Compete.

## Visual Direction
Primary style:
- Matte black
- White typography
- Gray secondary tones
- Neon blue accents
- Neon pink accents

Preferred look:
- Nike-inspired athletic visuals
- Premium sports-performance atmosphere
- High contrast
- Dark backgrounds
- Stadium lighting
- Chalk particles
- Shallow depth of field
- Clean layouts with spacing

Avoid:
- Clutter
- Generic subscription-box aesthetics
- Bright random colors
- Overly busy pages
- Cheap-looking graphics

## Website Structure Direction
Sections should feel cinematic and premium.

Preferred sections:
- Hero section
- Choose Your TrackBox
- Session Packs
- Product explanation pages
- Team integrations
- Athlete ecosystem
- Recovery systems
- Coaching systems
- Fuel and hydration systems

## Product Presentation
Products should be displayed as:
- Organized systems
- Performance tools
- Athlete preparation systems
- Recovery and competition support

Packaging should feel premium and intentional.
