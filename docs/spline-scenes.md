# TrackBox Spline Scenes

## Goal
Use Spline scenes to make the TrackBox website feel immersive, cinematic, and premium.

Spline should be used for:
- Homepage hero scenes
- Floating product scenes
- Orbiting TrackBox scenes
- Interactive product reveals
- Lighting environments
- Background motion

## Main Homepage Scene
Scene concept:
- Dark environment
- Floating TrackBox
- Camera movement tied to scroll
- Box opening animation
- Products orbiting around the box
- Lighting reacting to movement
- Transition into homepage content

## Visual Direction
Scenes should feel:
- Dark
- Premium
- Athletic
- Scientific
- Minimal
- High contrast
- Cinematic

## Preferred Scene Elements
Use:
- Floating products
- Matte black surfaces
- Neon blue accents
- Neon pink accent lighting
- Soft fog or haze
- Particle effects
- Depth of field
- Rim lighting
- Camera drift

Avoid:
- Cartoonish objects
- Overly bright colors
- Flat lighting
- Generic gradients
- Empty scenes

## Scene Types
### Hero Intro Scene
Main cinematic homepage scene.

### Floating Product Scene
Products slowly rotate or float with subtle movement.

### Session Pack Scene
Show PRE / DURING / POST products grouped together.

### Sponsor Scene
Featured partner products and logos integrated into premium environments.

### Apparel Scene
TrackBox apparel floating or displayed in premium lighting.

## Interaction Rules
Use:
- Scroll interaction
- Mouse movement parallax
- Slow camera drift
- Smooth transitions
- Product hover emphasis

Avoid aggressive or distracting movement.

## Technical Direction
Preferred stack:
- Spline for visual scene creation
- GSAP for scroll integration
- React integration if needed
- Lazy loading for performance

## Mobile Rules
Mobile versions should:
- Simplify scenes
- Reduce object count
- Reduce particles
- Maintain premium feel
- Prioritize performance

## Asset Rules
Use real uploaded assets whenever possible.

Preferred:
- Transparent PNG products
- Proper logo assets
- Real packaging renders
- Real box dimensions

## Codex Instructions
Codex should:
- Treat Spline scenes as major visual anchors
- Build layouts around them
- Maintain section continuity after scenes
- Avoid hard cuts into normal sections
- Keep the visual language consistent throughout the site
